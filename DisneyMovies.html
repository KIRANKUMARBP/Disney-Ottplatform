Bajarangi 2

In Bajrangi 2, the story picks up with Bajrangi embarking on a new journey filled with thrilling action, heartfelt moments, and powerful messages. The film explores themes of courage, humanity, and the importance of staying true to one's values. As Bajrangi faces new challenges and adversaries, he remains committed to his mission of bringing justice and peace to those around him.

Ghost

Ghost is a thrilling Kannada-language horror film that delves into the eerie and supernatural world of spirits and the unknown. Directed by M. G. Srinivas, who also plays the lead role, the film combines elements of horror, suspense, and drama to create a gripping narrative.

Mufti

Mufti is a gripping Kannada-language action thriller that delves into the dark and dangerous world of the underworld mafia. Directed by Narthan, the film stars Shiva Rajkumar and Srimurali in the lead roles, delivering powerful performances that drive the intense narrative.
